Artemis Capstone Project: Phase 1

Introduction

In the initial phase of the Artemis Capstone Project, our focus is on performing thorough reconnaissance to build a comprehensive profile of Artemis, Incorporated. As cybersecurity consultants, our team will employ a strategic blend of tools and methods, ranging from advanced Google searches and WHOIS lookups to social media analysis and DNS interrogation.

By harnessing these resources, we aim to understand Artemis deeply, ensuring a solid foundation for subsequent phases of the penetration test. This reconnaissance phase not only sets the stage for a successful engagement but also underscores our commitment to delivering tailored cybersecurity solutions that address Artemis' unique challenges and concerns. Let's embark on this crucial first step towards fortifying Artemis against emerging cyber threats.

Procedure

  1. Google Search: Use advanced Google searches to find information about Artemis.
  2. WHOIS Lookup: Look up domain registration details to understand Artemis' online presence.

Conclusion

This reconnaissance phase employs a variety of tools and methods, including the strategic utilization of numerous OSINT resources, to gather comprehensive information about Artemis.
